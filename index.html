Welcome to Revital Daycare

Located in the heart of Winnetka, CA, Revital Daycare is a licensed home-based early childhood program serving families with children ages 2-5. Founded by teacher Revital Edry, our program combines the warmth of a home environment with the structure and educational focus of a professional preschool.

We believe that the early years are the most formative, and we take that responsibility seriously. Our play-based, child-centered approach — inspired by Reggio Emilia principles — encourages children to explore, create, and discover at their own pace. Every activity in our daily routine is designed to support cognitive, social-emotional, physical, and language development.

With low child-to-staff ratios (6:1 for preschoolers), your child receives the individualized attention they deserve. We partner closely with parents through daily updates and open communication to ensure consistency between home and daycare.

Our curriculum includes literacy, math, science, art, music, and outdoor exploration — all taught through engaging, hands-on experiences. We also emphasize social-emotional learning, helping children develop empathy, self-regulation, and teamwork skills that form the foundation for lifelong success. Whether your child is just starting their educational journey or preparing for kindergarten, Revital Daycare provides the nurturing environment they need to thrive.
